OnCl - Edu. Cloud ?

Improved Student

A cloud-based Education Management System could be a boon for teachers who want to encourage collaboration of students who want to work together for group projects.

A gradual introduction of group work over the course of an entire year while involving students in as many group experiences as possible, is essential to guarantee that collaboration will be successful.


Easy

Online Educational Management System is easy to access because it’s not tied to an local machine or portable computer or device. Instead, all the information or data it’s managing is keep within the cloud.

The data can be accessed in educational institution using any computer. That work can be continued from where its left in a different system.

Access from

The software can be accessed from anywhere and at any time. A record of everything can be kept due to its easy accessibility. It also facilitates providing immediate information to all the stakeholders.

All they require is the credentials of the online education ERP.


Transparency With Parent

The cloud based ERP software, leads to the interaction with the parents as well. Parents can check on their wards from time to time and keep a track of their advances in the academic fields.

This leads to the transparency between the parents and the wards.
